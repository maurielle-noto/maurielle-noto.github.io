About Me

Hi, I'm Maurielle.

Just another nerd curious about what's going on upstairs.

I think I am quite lucky to have known what I have wanted to do from a young age. My planetary obsession began at age 5 reading my children's encyclopedia. It opened to the 'space facts' page, and here we are now!

I am currently a master's student in Earth, Atmospheric, and Planetary Sciences at MIT, where I also completed my undergraduate degree.

🌍 Travel & Outdoors

In the age where we spend the majority of our time staring at a screen and sitting at a desk, it is absolutely imperative to get outside and reconnect with our natural world. Growing up in Arizona, I never really saw 'green' until starting at MIT. Every day when the sun is shining, you can bet I am biking up to the nearest body of water or laying in the park.

🎨 Art

Having an artist as a mother really accelerated my ability to pick up drawing, painting, sewing, and photography. Not in that there is confined process to follow, but that there is beauty in everything and your art is whatever you want it to be. My current projects right now are water color paintings of the La Jolla Shores cliffs in California and a take on the 'All in the Golden Afternoon' scene from Alice in Wonderland.
